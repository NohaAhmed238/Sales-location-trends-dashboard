# Sales, Location & Trends Analysis Dashboards

An interactive Tableau reporting solution developed to analyze sales performance, geographic distribution, customer behavior, and business trends across the United States. Data was cleaned and preprocessed using Python before being visualized in Tableau. The project consists of three dashboards that provide insights into sales growth, regional performance, customer profitability, and future sales trends.

---

## Dashboard Previews

### Sales Dashboard 2017

![Sales Dashboard](Sales%20Dashboard.png)

The **Sales Dashboard 2017** provides an executive overview of business performance by comparing current and previous year metrics for Sales, Profit, and Quantity. It highlights year-over-year growth, monthly performance trends, top-performing products by profit, and sales distribution across product sub-categories. Interactive filters enable users to analyze performance by product, location, shipping mode, and region, helping identify key revenue drivers and profitable product lines.

---

### Location Dashboard 2017

![Location Dashboard](Location%20Dashboard.png)

The **Location Dashboard 2017** focuses on geographical sales analysis across the United States. It visualizes sales performance by state, city, region, and shipping method, allowing users to identify high-performing markets and regional opportunities. Interactive maps display sales concentration and order distribution, while regional comparisons provide valuable insights into customer demand and market performance across different locations.

---

### Trend Dashboard 2017

![Trend Dashboard](Trend%20Dashboard.png)

The **Trend Dashboard 2017** analyzes sales and profit trends over time and includes forecasting visualizations to support future planning. It tracks monthly and weekly sales performance, compares sales against profit trends, identifies top customers by profitability, and evaluates category-level sales distribution. Forecasting components help uncover future growth opportunities and support data-driven strategic decision-making.
